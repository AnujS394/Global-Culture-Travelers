Mumbai – The City of Dreams & Diverse Celebrations

Mumbai, the financial and film capital of India, is a melting pot of traditions, arts, and global cultures. From Ganesh Chaturthi’s grandeur to Kala Ghoda’s creativity, every festival here reflects the city's vibrant spirit.

Major Cultural & Religious Festivals

🐘 Ganesh Chaturthi

When: August/September

Where: Lalbaug, Girgaon, Siddhivinayak Temple

Mumbai’s grandest celebration. Gigantic idols of Lord Ganesha are installed in pandals, with 10 days of devotion, cultural programs, and the final visarjan procession through marine roads and beaches.

🎨 Kala Ghoda Arts Festival

When: February

Where: Kala Ghoda District, Fort Area

Mumbai’s iconic art fest brings together installations, dance, music, theatre, literature, heritage walks, and food. A must-attend for art lovers and tourists.

🎬 Mumbai Film Festival (MAMI)

When: October/November

Where: Theatres across Mumbai

A globally recognized film celebration hosted by MAMI. It showcases independent, international, and Indian cinema with panel discussions and red carpet evenings.

💃 Navratri & Dandiya Nights

When: September/October

Where: Goregaon, Borivali, Malad Grounds

Dazzling dandiya nights and garba celebrations filled with color, music, and dance. Dress up and join Mumbai’s festive pulse.

⛪ Bandra Fair

When: September (after Mother Mary’s feast)

Where: Mount Mary Church, Bandra

A week-long Christian festival with prayers, market stalls, sweets, and traditional games. Celebrates unity across communities.

🕯️ Diwali & Deepotsav

Where: Marine Drive, Juhu, Housing Societies

Homes, roads, and landmarks shimmer with diyas and lights. Crackers, rangoli, and community dinners make Diwali magical in Mumbai.
