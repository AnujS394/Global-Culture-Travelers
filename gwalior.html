🏰 Gwalior – A Royal City of Ragas & Heritage

Gwalior, the historic heart of Madhya Pradesh, blends majestic forts, musical legends, and timeless traditions. Once the cradle of Hindustani classical music, this city’s soul still resonates with ragas, royal echoes, and rich culture.

🎉 Major Cultural Festivals & Events

🎼 Tansen Samaroh

When: December (Annually)

Where: Tansen Tomb Complex, Gwalior Fort

A prestigious classical music festival honoring Mian Tansen, one of Akbar's Navratnas and father of Hindustani classical music. Top vocalists, instrumentalists, and gharanas perform over five soulful nights. A spiritual musical pilgrimage.

📖 Gwalior Gaurav Diwas

When: June 1

Where: Maharaj Bada, Jai Vilas Palace Grounds

Celebrating the pride and history of Gwalior with parades, heritage walks, school performances, and honoring city icons.

🎭 Scindia Sangeet Mahotsav

When: October/November

Where: Scindia School, Jai Vilas Palace

An elegant evening supported by the royal family, showcasing classical and fusion music in a regal ambiance.

🗳️ Diwali & Deepotsav

Where: Gwalior Fort, Sun Temple

Gwalior Fort glows during Diwali with diyas and lights. Temples host spiritual bhajans, processions, and fireworks.

🎨 Craft & Handloom Fair

When: February/March

Where: Mela Ground, Padav

Showcasing Chanderi & Zardozi, tribal art, regional handlooms, folk dance and food.

🍲 Gwalior Food & Heritage Festival

When: December / January

Where: Maharaj Bada, Fort View Ground

Enjoy Gwalior’s royal cuisine with music and storytelling: Bedai, Kachori, Imarti, Khichdi, Laddoo & more.

🎬 Rangilo Gwalior Film & Art Festival

When: Varies

Where: Auditoriums, film clubs

A platform for regional cinema, art, installations, and photography focused on Gwalior’s cultural lens.

🥘 Taste of Gwalior

  • Bedai with Aloo Sabzi – Morning spicy crisp
  • Imarti & Ladoo – Sweet syrupy classics
  • Kachori-Chutney – Flavor-packed street treat
  • Sabudana Khichdi – Light and loved
  • Kesari Doodh – Royal saffron milk at night stalls
